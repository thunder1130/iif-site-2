ALL ABOUT INCREDIBLE IDEAL FURNITURE

I opened my studio back in 2004. What started as a  small-scale workshop, soon grew into a full-time business specializing in custom, handcrafted goods. While I love creating new pieces, I also derive immense satisfaction from restoring old items and making them whole and functional. Around here at Incredible Ideal Furniture, I plan and fabricate, fix and reestablish wooden furnishings. I have been known to take a heap of "kindling" made by a mover and reconstruct a table that looked all around great. Nothing makes me more joyful than giving an exemplary old piece a renewed purpose for carrying on with life.
I fix tables, seats, sofas, closets, work areas and the sky is the limit from there. I can take a very much cherished piece and make it strong once more, I remove the ramshackle, fix the "unfixable" and makeover the drained looking.
